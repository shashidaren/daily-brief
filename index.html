Malaysia News

On August 27, 2025 a minor 3.2‑magnitude earthquake struck near the town of Segamat in Johor at 8:59 a.m. local time. The quake, centred about 18 km south of Segamat along the Mersing Fault Zone, did not cause any deaths or injuries, according to Malaysia’s Meteorological Department【512616299735638†L1537-L1583】. Officials reported the tremor was likely an aftershock and urged residents to remain calm. Minor earthquakes like this are occasionally felt in Peninsular Malaysia but rarely cause significant damage.

World News

Heavy rains from Tropical Storm Kajiki brought extensive flooding and landslides across Vietnam and Thailand. Authorities reported at least eight deaths (seven in Vietnam and one in Thailand).
